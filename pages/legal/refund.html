Our Promise

22PM stands behind every workflow we build. If your automation doesn't save measurable time or improve your business metrics within 7 days, we refund your deposit — no questions asked.

7-Day Risk-Free Trial

Every new client receives a 7-day free trial period starting from the moment their workflow is deployed. During this period:

  • You pay a 50% deposit to begin work
  • We build and deploy your custom workflow
  • You use it for 7 days in your live environment
  • If it doesn't save time, we refund 100% of your deposit
  • If it works, you pay the remaining 50%

How to Request a Refund

Simply email hello@22pm.work within the 7-day trial period with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Company Name]". Include a brief explanation of why the workflow didn't meet expectations. We'll process your refund within 3 business days.

What Gets Refunded

The full 50% deposit is refundable. No partial refunds, no processing fees, no "restocking" charges. If you're not happy, you get every penny back.

After the Trial Period

Once the 7-day trial ends and the final 50% payment is made, the project is considered complete. Refunds after the trial period are handled on a case-by-case basis for ongoing retainer services. Monthly retainers can be cancelled with 30 days written notice.

Exceptions

Refunds do not apply if: the client fails to provide required access or information within 5 business days, the client materially changes the scope after work begins, or the client's third-party tool accounts are not set up or are suspended.
